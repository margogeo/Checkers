Checkers

Rules

    Rules

  • Click on a checker and then on a cell you want to move it to
  • Basic checker can be moved on 1 cell diagonally towards opposite player's side
  • Click on a cell next to a checker you want to take
  • Basic checkers can only take a checker near them on diagonal in any direction
  • Player have to take all the checkers available in one turn
  • Reaching the furthest line makes a checker a King
  • A King can move in any direction diagonally, but only one time in a turn
  • Rule number three is same for Kings
  • Game ends when one of the players runs out of checkers
